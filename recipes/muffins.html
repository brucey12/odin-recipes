Desciption

Theese muffins slap anytime and are easy to make. What more could you ask for?

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • ½ tsp ground ginger
  • ½ salt
  • ⅓ cup ground flaxseed
  • 3 large eggs, room temperature
  • ½ sugar, packed down
  • ¼ cup honey
  • ⅓ melted coconut oil
  • ⅓ unsweetened smooth applesauce
  • 1 tsp orange zest
  • ⅓ cup orange juice
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups shredded carrots (about 4 large)
  • 1 cup grated apple (about 1 large)
  • ½ cup raisins
  • ½ chopped pecans
