Baked Curry Chicken Thighs

Description

Chicken thighs are often on sale at my local grocery store, and this is a recipe I find the whole family enjoys. Hopefully yours does too.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbs oil
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 6 chicken thighs
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 3 tsp curry poweder
  • ½ tsp cayenne
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 2 carrots, peeled and sliced on diagonal
  • 1 ½ cups chicken stock
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • ¼ heavy cream
  • ¾ cup frozen peas
  • Cooked basmati rice; for serving
